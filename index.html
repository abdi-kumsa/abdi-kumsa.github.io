From Aerospace to Cyber Defense

My dream started with looking up—I wanted to be an Aerospace Engineer. I worked as a shopkeeper to support my education, eventually scoring in the top 0.025% nationally.

When the door to international aerospace programs closed, I didn't stop. I pivoted. I realized that Computer Science offers a different way to launch: not rockets, but digital systems that protect our nation.

A Vision for African Education

My ultimate goal extends beyond code. I have a vision to establish a continent-level university in collaboration with the African Union. I want to create a sanctuary where Africa's brightest minds can pursue world-class education without the barriers of international competition—building the opportunity for my brothers and sisters that I once fought for.
